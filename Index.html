Functional Testing of Amazon Main Features

1. Search Functionality Testing

Verify that the search bar correctly displays search results based on keywords entered. Test various scenarios including single keywords, phrases, and misspelled words.

2. Product Page Testing

Test the product page layout, images, product description, and pricing accuracy. Ensure that "Add to Cart" and "Buy Now" buttons work as expected.

3. Cart and Checkout Testing

Verify that items added to the cart are displayed accurately. Test the checkout process, including adding/removing items, applying promo codes, and selecting shipping options.
